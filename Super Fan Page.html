Biography  

In Chino Hills, California, on August 22, 2001, LaMelo was born. His parents, LaVar and Tina Ball, were also collegiate basketball players. Lonzo, one of LaMelo's brothers, is an NBA point guard, while LiAngelo is also a former professional player. Ball is a cast member of the reality series "Ball in the Family" on Facebook Watch, which depicts the Ball family's daily lives. The Melo Ball 1 is a signature sneaker for LaMelo from The Big Baller Brand, a sportswear firm established by Ball's family. He has a signature shoe, making him the youngest athlete in history to do so. Ball played professionally in Australia and Lithuania before joining the NBA. Ball decided to play basketball abroad rather than attend college.
